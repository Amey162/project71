Ryan International School



Ryan International School (RIS), Chandigarh is an English medium kindergarten-class XII school affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE). Promoted in 2003, RIS Chandigarh is part of the Ryan International Group of Institutions (RIGI) founded by Dr. Augustine F. Pinto and Grace Pinto.
